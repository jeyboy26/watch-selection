The read

Overlap to know

Citizen's Sport Luxury label covers two unrelated watches — the AT8260 atomic chrono (C$740) and the BM7660 integrated three-hander (C$556). The two Zenshins look identical; the C$100 step from the NJ0180 (C$895) to the NK5020 (C$995) just buys the 60-hour 8322 movement over the older 8213. The two PRC 100 Solars (C$675) differ only in dial colour.

If you're choosing

The PR 100 chrono (C$550) is the value outlier. The Gentleman is the one-watch-does-everything pick. The Fugu is the only true swimmer — currently sold out on the CA site. The Tsuki-Yomi is the only genuine step-up, in titanium with an atomic moonphase; everything else sits in the C$550–1,050 sport/casual band.

On pricing

Figures are as shown on the sites today. Tissot are list price; the two Citizen 20% off tags and the Fugu's sold-out status will drift, so re-check on the page before buying.
